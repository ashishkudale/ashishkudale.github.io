Offline Expense Tracker — Privacy Policy

We do not collect, store, or share personal data on our servers. All data you enter (profiles, categories, transactions) is stored locally on your device. The app does not require an account or internet connection. If you choose to export data (e.g., CSV in future versions), the file is created locally and you control how and where it’s shared.
