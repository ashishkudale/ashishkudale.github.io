KhetBook — Privacy Policy

We do not collect, store, or share personal data on our servers. All data you enter (crop seasons, expenses, harvest details, sale records) is stored locally on your device. The app does not require an account or internet connection. If you choose to export data (e.g., CSV or PDF in future versions), the file is created locally and you control how and where it's shared.
