Malegaon Aagar — Privacy Policy

We do not collect, store, or share any personal data. The app works entirely offline — all timetable data (bus routes, stops, schedules) is bundled with the app and stored locally on your device. The app does not require an account, login, or internet connection to function.

Favourites you save and your language preference (English / Marathi) are stored locally on your device using standard Android storage and are never transmitted to any server.
